My Journey

Background

I was born in a farm called Rhino Lodge in Secunda "Mpumalanga" and that is where I spent most of my childhood, and that is the place I still call home. When I finished primary school, I decided to move to Duduza in the Eastrand, to stay with my grandmother and attend high school there, since the schools in the farms were disadvantaged back then and I wanted better education for myself. I then matriculated in 2013 and was amongst the top 3 best performing students in our school and was awarded a bursay to go further my studies. In 2014 I took a gap year before going to university since I could not get space in the engineering programs I had applied to.

Education

In 2015 I enrolled for a Bing Electrical & Electronic at the University of Johannesburg. I finished my studies in 2020. During my time at the University of Johannesburg, I also did a certificate for Artificial Intelligence in the 4IR and a CISCO certificate for Introduction to Cyber Security. I have also done online courses in Udemy for programming.

Experience

In 2021 I worked for a solar energy company, where I worked as an Electrical Engineer, responsible for solar plant designs, monitoring and maintenance. I worked in small projects like residential projects as well as in large projects like mines, hospitals and large corporate and hotel buildings. I was also involved in completion of technical proposals for tendering. Before that I worked in Eskom Kusile Power Station in Witbank during holidays when I was still at university. I also worked in a game reserve during my gap year in 2014. I am currently working for CapaCiTi as a Junior Cloud App Developer.
